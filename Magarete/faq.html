  • question_answerI am from other department then CSE can I still submit the article for the magazine?

    Yes, why not! Our magazine is all about its diversity. Any student or a Faculty member from every respective department of the PARUL UNIVERSITY can submit their articles for the magazine by choosing anyone or both of the following categories that is 1) Technical & 2) Non-Technical* (Stories, Poems, Trolls, paintings & photos*), based on the themes of every particular edition as declared on the of launch date of a peculiar edition.

  • question_answerI am alumni of parul university (gtu) batch can I submit article for the magazine?

    Yes, you can submit your articles for any one or both of the categories such as 1).Technical & 2). Non-Technical, for any edition of the magazine irrespective of any department to which you belonged during your academic duration at PARUL UNIVERSITY previously.
